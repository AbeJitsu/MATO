Florida Real Estate Commission Pre-License

The course covers a range of topics including real estate law, property rights, contracts, mortgages, appraisal, and brokerage activities. 

Introduction

This 63-hour course fulfills the education requirement to become a licensed real estate sales associate in the state of Florida. Whether you’re starting a new career or seeking a flexible opportunity, this course provides you with the essential knowledge and skills to succeed in the real estate industry.

Course Objectives

You’ll learn the core principles and business fundamentals of real estate, including:

  • Sales associate responsibilities
  • Contracts and agreements
  • Title closings and escrow
  • Foreclosures and deeds
  • Easements and property rights
  • Ethics and professional standards
  • And much more.
